About Sentiment Analysis

Sentiment analysis, also known as opinion mining, is the process of determining the sentiment expressed in a piece of text, such as a review, tweet, or comment. It involves analyzing the text to identify and classify the expressed sentiment as positive, negative, or neutral. Sentiment analysis can be useful for understanding public opinion, customer feedback, and social media trends.

How It Works

Data Preprocessing

Analysis

The input text is analyzed to determine its sentiment (positive, negative, or neutral).

The analysis result is displayed, indicating the sentiment of the input text.
